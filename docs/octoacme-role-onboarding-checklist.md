## Purpose
Provide a standardized checklist to onboard team members into specific roles on OctoAcme projects, ensuring they have access, context, and clarity about their responsibilities.

## General Onboarding (All Roles)

- [ ] Receive project overview and access to project documentation
- [ ] Review [Project Management Overview](octoacme-project-management-overview.md)
- [ ] Review [Roles and Personas](octoacme-roles-and-personas.md) document
- [ ] Introduction to project team members and stakeholders
- [ ] Access to communication channels (Slack, Teams, email distribution lists)
- [ ] Access to project management tools (GitHub Projects, Jira, etc.)
- [ ] Review project charter and current roadmap
- [ ] Understand escalation paths and who to contact for help

## Role-Specific Onboarding

### Product Owner
- [ ] Review product vision and strategy documents
- [ ] Access to product backlog and prioritization tools
- [ ] Meet with key stakeholders and understand their needs
- [ ] Review success metrics and product KPIs
- [ ] Understand budget and resource constraints
- [ ] Access to customer feedback channels and analytics tools
- [ ] Review upcoming milestones and release plans
- [ ] Understand approval authority and decision-making boundaries

### Business Analyst
- [ ] Access to requirements management tools
- [ ] Review existing requirements documentation
- [ ] Meet with Product Owner to understand product vision
- [ ] Identify key stakeholders for requirements gathering
- [ ] Review business process documentation
- [ ] Access to user research and customer feedback
- [ ] Understand documentation standards and templates
- [ ] Review acceptance criteria guidelines

### UX/UI Designer
- [ ] Access to design tools (Figma, Sketch, Adobe XD, etc.)
- [ ] Review design system and component libraries
- [ ] Understand accessibility standards and requirements
- [ ] Meet with Product Owner and Business Analyst to understand user needs
- [ ] Access to user research data and analytics
- [ ] Review brand guidelines and visual design standards
- [ ] Understand design review and approval process
- [ ] Set up collaboration with development team for design handoffs

### Release Manager
- [ ] Access to CI/CD pipelines and deployment tools
- [ ] Review release schedule and upcoming deployments
- [ ] Understand environment topology (dev, staging, production)
- [ ] Access to monitoring and alerting tools
- [ ] Review rollback procedures and incident response playbooks
- [ ] Meet with QA team to understand testing processes
- [ ] Review quality gates and release criteria
- [ ] Understand change management and approval workflows

### Change Control Coordinator
- [ ] Access to change management system
- [ ] Review change management policy and procedures
- [ ] Understand Change Advisory Board (CAB) schedule and participants
- [ ] Review current open and recent change requests
- [ ] Understand risk assessment framework
- [ ] Meet with Project Manager and technical leads
- [ ] Review emergency change procedures
- [ ] Access to audit and compliance documentation

### Developer
- [ ] Repository access and development environment setup
- [ ] Review coding standards and contribution guidelines
- [ ] Understand branching strategy and PR workflow
- [ ] Access to CI/CD pipelines and test environments
- [ ] Review architecture documentation
- [ ] Meet with technical lead for codebase walkthrough
- [ ] Understand Definition of Done and code review expectations
- [ ] Set up local development environment and run tests

### Product Manager
- [ ] Review product strategy and market positioning
- [ ] Access to product analytics and metrics dashboards
- [ ] Meet with engineering leads to understand technical capabilities
- [ ] Review competitive analysis and market research
- [ ] Understand customer segments and personas
- [ ] Access to feedback channels (support tickets, surveys, etc.)
- [ ] Review roadmap planning process and timelines
- [ ] Understand budget and resource allocation process

### Project Manager
- [ ] Access to project tracking tools and dashboards
- [ ] Review project charter and scope documentation
- [ ] Meet with all key stakeholders and team members
- [ ] Understand resource allocation and team capacity
- [ ] Review risk register and current issues
- [ ] Access to status reporting templates
- [ ] Understand escalation procedures and decision-making authority
- [ ] Review communication plan and meeting cadence

## Post-Onboarding

- [ ] Schedule 30-day check-in to address questions and gaps
- [ ] Ensure ongoing access to documentation and updates
- [ ] Provide feedback on onboarding process for continuous improvement
- [ ] Confirm understanding of role responsibilities and expectations

## Onboarding Owner
- Project Manager or designated team lead is responsible for ensuring onboarding checklist completion
- Track onboarding progress and address any gaps or blockers
- Schedule regular check-ins during the first 30 days
